Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the GlowSync + Mindbody integration.

How do I connect GlowSync to Mindbody?

Connecting takes about 5 minutes. Click "Connect Mindbody" in your GlowSync dashboard, log into your Mindbody account, and authorize the connection. GlowSync uses secure OAuth authentication, so your Mindbody password is never shared with us.

What data does GlowSync sync from Mindbody?

GlowSync syncs appointments (upcoming and completed), client contact information, and treatment/service history. All syncing happens in real-time, so new appointments appear in GlowSync within seconds of being booked.

Is the Mindbody integration secure?

Yes, GlowSync uses Mindbody's official OAuth 2.0 authentication. Your Mindbody password is never shared with or stored by GlowSync. You can revoke access at any time. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

How quickly do appointments sync?

Appointments sync in real-time. When a client books, reschedules, or cancels in Mindbody, GlowSync is updated within seconds. This ensures your automated reminders and follow-ups always have accurate data.

Does GlowSync replace Mindbody's messaging?

GlowSync complements Mindbody by adding advanced SMS automation not available in Mindbody's native tools—treatment-specific follow-ups, rebooking campaigns, review requests, and more. Many spas use both but rely on GlowSync for retention-focused messaging.

What if I disconnect Mindbody later?

If you disconnect, GlowSync stops syncing new data but retains your campaign history and analytics. You can reconnect anytime and syncing resumes automatically. Your templates and settings are preserved.

Is there an extra cost for the Mindbody integration?

No, the Mindbody integration is included free with all GlowSync plans. There are no setup fees, no per-sync charges, and no hidden costs. You only pay your standard GlowSync subscription.
